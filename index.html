Projects

Master Thesis · Ongoing
Vibration Analysis of Mechanical Drive in Switchgear

Detecting mechanical looseness in medium-voltage switchgear drive assemblies using advanced signal processing. Accelerometers are mounted on the drive mechanism to capture vibration data during switching operations. Signal decomposition via Hilbert-Huang Transform (HHT) and Empirical Mode Decomposition (EMD) extracts intrinsic mode functions, enabling detection of subtle structural anomalies that conventional FFT/PSD methods may miss — improving reliability assessment before costly field failures occur.

Signal Processing HHT / EMD FFT & PSD Vibration Analysis Python Reliability Engineering
